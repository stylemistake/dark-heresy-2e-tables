Basic descriptions
Toughness test
9
 Conditions exist in a variety of extremities:Toughness test frequency: Whenever a character spends at least a turn in any of the described environmental conditions, he must immediately make a Toughness test. Modify the difficulty by the number specific to the condition. He must continue making toughness tests continuosly, making one test per Test Period. Temporarily leaving the conditions pauses the test period, but does not cancel it; to fully restore the timer, the character has to spend a full Test Period without being affected by the noted environmental condition.
10
- Normal conditions support human life without additional precautions. It may get cool during the night, or hot during the day, but this does not affect the mechanics in any way. Normal condition planets are very desired colonisation targets.
11
12
- Inhospitable conditions require basic precautions to support human life. However, these conditions do not threaten human life immediately, and short periods of exposure does not cause any harm. Many inhospitable planets have been colonised and reached prosperity.
13
14
- Borderline conditions pose immediate harm. Although they do not kill outright, borderline conditions will drain human life in a matter of hours, and standard precautions will buy little time. Gear and suits are usually required to survive medium durations on borderline planets. Colonies only appear in cases of unusual interest, and retreat under domes or other sealed structures.
15
16
17
- Extreme conditions cause a loss of conciousness in seconds, and a loss of life in a minute, if not less. Standard environmental gear is not enough to survive in these conditions, and even the most specialised gear guarantees survival for only a short time. Colonies on these plates are very rare, but sometimes they do exist - under heavy, deep sealed doors.
18
Acclimatization: If the character ever scores 5 degrees of success on an environment resistance test, be becomes immune to its effects for 1 day. This is cancelled if the conditions get worse, or if the character downgrades his precautions.

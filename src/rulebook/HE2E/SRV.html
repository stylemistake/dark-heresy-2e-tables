99
Acolyte Cells often have a cleric with them during their investigations and these clerics can perform the services of the Ecclesiarchy for the Acolytes he is with. To perform a service, the cleric makes a Common Lore (Imperial Creed) test at the difficulty listed on the table below, and performs an appropriate ritual (usually 10 minutes or longer). Only characters that have the Pure Faith talent can make this test. If the test is failed, no more sermons can be performed until the next day. If this service is requisitioned with influence, the test is passed automatically.
